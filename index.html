What we do?

We provide below services to our customers.

Mobile Platform

Meal Magic application is available on Android Play store and Apple App stores, customers can download the application to subscribe to their preferred menus and caterers can register to create their custom menus on the platform.

Subscribe to Healthy Meals

Targeted to employees and students who have a need for a healthy daily meal service. They can select from a wide range of menus from nearby caterers and get their food delivered to them.

Providing customers to Caterers

We with the help of our platform want to help caterers grow their reach by providing customers from wide range of locations, this will help them increase their revenue and quality.
